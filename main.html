Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu. It's famous for its numerous classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Kinkaku-ji: The Golden Pavilion
  2. Fushimi Inari Taisha: The shrine of a thousand gates
  3. Kiyomizu-dera: A historic temple that offers stunning views over Kyoto
  4. Gion: The famous geisha district
  5. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: An atmospheric bamboo forest

Must-try Foods

  • Kaiseki: The art form that balances the taste, texture, appearance, and colors of food
  • Yudofu: Tofu hot pot
  • Saba-zushi: Mackerel on rice
  • Matcha: Green tea
  • Yuba: Tofu skin
